<td><h2>Getting paid shouldn't cost you a relationship.</h2></td>

I've seen too many freelancers, founders, and office managers lose sleep over unpaid invoices. Or worse, damage client relationships just trying to get paid. After 10+ years building software for others, I decided to tackle this one myself.

That's why I'm building [**Yetty**](https://yetty.framer.website/). It's an invoice follow-up tool that's smart, human, and kind. The colleague you wish you had, the one who handles the awkward conversations so you don't have to.
