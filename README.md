## Getting paid shouldn't cost you a relationship.

I've seen too many freelancers, founders, and office managers lose sleep over unpaid invoices. Or worse, damage client relationships just trying to get paid. After 10+ years building software for others, I decided to tackle this one myself.

That's why I'm building [**Yetty**](https://yetty.framer.website/).

### Shaping Storybook

I was a **core team member of [Storybook](https://github.com/storybookjs/storybook)** from 2019 to 2022. I shipped features, improved CI/CD pipelines, and helped shape the roadmap of a tool used by thousands of teams. That's where I learned what it takes to build something people actually rely on.

---

### Making things run at Gravitee

I spent 3 years at [Gravitee](https://www.gravitee.io/), working on their core API Management platform. I shipped new features, merged tightly coupled repositories into a monorepo, set up visual regression testing with Chromatic, and reworked the release process to go from 1 day to 20 minutes. I also revamped CI pipelines to integrate all automated tests across projects.
